******** Code of Conduct *********** 

 

* The team is expected to work together in the coursework in a professional academic manner. 

* Each member is expected to participate and be proactive.

* It is imperative to be present in each team meeting unless there is an absence due to sickness or emergency. 

* Everyone in the team is expected to provide support and help, and also expected to seek assistance in case they have any doubts or questions.
